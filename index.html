Welcome Indiana County Humane Society employees!

This website is designed to help you learn about the Indiana County Humane Society daily tasks and procedures. Check out the different pages in the navigation bar above or click the links below. You will review the eLearning modules for the Shelter Layout, Dog Team Training, Cat Team Training, and Pet Adoption Process. Each page contains content that will provide training for each of these areas. This training is intended to be self-paced and engaging so take your time and have fun!
